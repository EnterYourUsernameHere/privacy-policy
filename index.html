Privacy Policy for Beer Trekker – Australia

Last updated: 8 January 2026

Beer Trekker – Australia (the “App”) is an Android application owned and operated by Barry Hart.

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and handled when you use the App.

1. Information Collection and Use

The App may request access to your device’s location in order to display nearby pubs, venues, and points of interest.

Location information is used solely within the App to provide location-based functionality and is not transmitted to external servers controlled by the developer.

The App does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

2. Data Storage

Any data generated by the App is stored locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to share it. The developer does not maintain a backend system that stores personal user data.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services such as mapping providers or publicly available data sources to deliver features. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

4. Children’s Information

Beer Trekker – Australia is intended for adult users and is not designed for children under the age of 18.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
